FAQ

How to order?
Reach us through any of our social media platforms so we can freely
discuss your requests in the commission.

How do I request a commission?
After choosing which style yoy want,
provide an idea and a reference photo of how you want your commission to be.
The artist will update you from time to time so you can monitor the progress.

What is the mode of payment?
We accept payments through Gcash, Paypal, and Unionbank.

When do I need to pay?
50% down payment should be done to secure your slot and avoid joy reservers.

How much should I pay for this commission?
It ranges from Php 500 - Php 3000 but depends on how detailed the work is,
please communicate with us so we can discuss about the pricing.

For customized orders, how long does it usually take to finish?
1-2 weeks depending on the complexity and how detailed the commissioned work is.

What happens if I cancel my commission?
Cancelation of commission is possible, however, the downpayment is non-refundable
as it serves as a payment for the initial work that the artist did.

Will the artist retain rights to the commissioned artwork?
Yes

Can I use my commissioned work for commercial purposes?
Yes
